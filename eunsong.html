Composer & Sound Artist

Eunsong Shin is a composer and sound artist whose practice centers on transforming personal perception into sound. Rooted in autobiographical narratives and sensory experience, her work explores listening as a site of memory, embodiment, and spatial awareness.

She works with electroacoustic media, AI, spatial sound, field recording, and sound installation.
